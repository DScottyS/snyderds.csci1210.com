Calembel, Gondor

I hail from a small town that used to be located in Gondor, near the river Ciril. My town is not known for much, but we used to be one of the biggest suppliers of wheat and milk to Minas Tirith! Unfortunately, all the town is known for these days is getting destroyed by a bunch of ghosts during the War of the Ring.

Education

All my education, like many of those who grew up in Calembel, comes from the town's oldest man, hairy Harry. He taught us in a barn I learned many things during those days, such as how to count chickens and groom horses (still not sure when this one will come into play but im sure it will be handy some day). I graduated from the barn in the spring of 3014 and immediately became a farmhand for balding Bradford, the town's least popular farmer (he smelled strongly of petrichor and elderberries).
